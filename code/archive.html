My Archive is a non-linear collection of creative works across mediums and artistic endeavors toward honesty and connection. This project explores a continual research interest: how do we achieve connection over the internet? Is communication design commodity or expression?

Overview

Each entry in the archive is presented within an iframe element, which initially displays as a generic advertisement. Interaction with the frame reveals the underlying work— photography, art, music... and so on.

Its structure draws on the visual vocabulary of early 2000s web portals— sidebars, banners, and inline ads as a conduit for expression. Is the experience overstimulating or just enough to keep someone interested in lieu of the endless scroll?

Navigation

Lace Curtains ✕

“There are times when the crisp white of freshly laundered bed linen can seem to speak. When that pure cotton fabric grazes her bare flesh, just there, it seems to tell her something. You are a noble person. Your sleep is clean, and the fact of your living is nothing to be ashamed of. Such is the strange comfort she receives, at that in-between time when sleep borders wakefulness, when that crisp cotton bedsheet brushes her skin.”

Han Kang
